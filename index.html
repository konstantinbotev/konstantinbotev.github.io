Konstantin Botev

Author | Policy Analyst | Systems Researcher

Konstantin Botev is a Bulgarian author and policy analyst working across institutional continuity, civilizational memory, material-form theory, speculative fiction, and constraint-based models of emergence.

Projects

MNEMOSYNE

A literary science fiction project about civilizational memory, archive systems, recovery, and the fragile capacity to understand evidence already present in the world.

Structure and Emergence: Lithogenism and Form

A methodological framework for figurative stone sculpture, focused on the interaction between material structure, interpretation, and emergent form.

Continuity Index Framework

A diagnostic model for making gradual institutional erosion under continuity deficits empirically observable.

Bulgaria 2126

A long-horizon model of state viability under demographic, territorial, institutional, and continuity stress.

The Constraint-First Hypothesis

A research programme exploring whether relational constraints may precede metric, object, and spatial structure.
